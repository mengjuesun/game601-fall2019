Game Modification: The Number Guessing Game
This is the first assignment of this class, I updated the traditional number guessing game to make it becomes more playable. Allows players to guess numbers through ten different math problems instead of clueless. This also requires players to have certain mathematical knowledge, so it is also an advanced puzzle game .0

(Midterm) Non-Digital Game: The War of Troy
This game is a board game about Troy war. It is based on the Troy war of Homer's lliad. Players are divided into two camps. Cards are composed of character cards and skill cards. Players of different characters perform the behavior shown by the dice by throwing four dice each round, and purchase skill cards to cooperate with the character's special skills to kill enemies or protect teammates. At the same time, a map is added to the game, which is the march element, which is closer to the real war.
PLAY
Visual Novel or Interactive Fiction: Rescue Milady
This exercise is a Twine game. The story of this game is based on Three Musketeers of Dumas. In the game, players need to play Felton, he was deceived by Milady and decided to rescue her from prison. Players need to make multiple choices about the ways to rescue Milady. Be careful, the wrong choice will directly lead to failure.
